Why choose

TAYLOR'S INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

At Taylor's International School (TIS), we are constantly keeping ahead to provide relevant education for our students so that they are ready for the possibilities of tomorrow.

At TIS, we provide exceptional education experiences that make learning joyful, meaningful and challenging. We nurture our students to be well-rounded individuals who not only excel academically but has good character, know their purpose and have the essential soft skills, confidence and knowledge to succeed and be ready for the possibilities of tomorrow.

Our Education

  • We deliver ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE
  • We develop CHARACTER
  • We build strong foundation in 3 LANGUAGES (English, Bahasa Malaysia and Mandarin)

Our Approach

  • Distinctive teaching and learning with Quantum Learning
  • Soft skills and character development
  • A nurturing environment for learning
Our Student Outcomes

We are committed towards achieving the following attributes in each of our students. These qualities are firmly embedded in the learning and behavior of TIS students.

Respectful & Caring Individuals

Caring learners who have a healthy respect for self, others and the environment

Resilient Individuals

Confident, articulate and analytical learners who can embrace change in an ever- evolving environment

Passionate Learners

A passion for learning that opens doors of opportunity and pathways to success

Global Citizens

Globally aware and make a difference by contributing positively to society

Responsible Leaders

Productive, community role models who are able to inspire and lead others
