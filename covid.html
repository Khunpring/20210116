Information and Updates Regarding COVID-19: Our campus may be closed, but learning most certainly continues!
Online Learning
The New Norm
Online Learning

March 2020 : When the Movement Control Order (MCO) was announced, the school has adapted learning to the online platforms, innovating the way teachers teach and the way the students learn.

We understand the emotional challenge parents are facing with regards to ensuring their children are safe and happy in school, hence it is our aim to make sure these rules are followed on a daily basis.

The New Norm

As society recovers from the Covid-19 pandemic, we have a responsibility to make sure we adhere to the government’s SOP to ensure the health and safety of our students.

Using the Quantum Learning System, online lessons are creatively designed by teachers to facilitate meaningful learning in ways to increase students’ participation, focus, and to enhance learning outcomes.

These online lessons are always interactive and engaging.
